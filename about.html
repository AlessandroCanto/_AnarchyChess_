About AnarchyChess

Who we are

The AnarchyChess team is made of two friends who are members of the AnarchyChess subreddit. One day, we came up with the idea of making an AnarchyChess website to expand the community and have fun with new rules. A few months later, this website was born.

Here at AnarchyChess, our mission is to connect the AnarchyChess community through collaborative entertainment. We hope to be a place where AnarchyChess community members can have fun together and enjoy all this community has to offer.
