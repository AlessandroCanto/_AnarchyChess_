Anarchy Chess Rules

AnarchyChess has many rules, with new rules being added or existing rules being changed every week. Whether you got confused by something you saw in a game, or are just interested in what new rules exist, this is the first place you should consult.

En Passant

This rule is a classic, with a pawn being able to capture any pawn that jumps two squares directly beside it the move after. However, when capturing En Passant to the left, the capturing pawn now moves back-right one square instead of forward-left.

Knight boost

Normally, when promoting a pawn people choose to make a queen. Sometimes a rook or bishop to avoid stalemate. But knights are often underappreciated. So, promoting to a knight is now forced.

Rookless Rooks

Rooks, well, aren't really rooks. They are unable to move, except for jumping all the way across the board to capture the opponents rook on the same file. If rooks are still on the board, though, some stalemates may be prevented. And this does mean no castling.

Short-range Bishops

Tired of being snipped by bishops across the board? Well, bishops now can only move 1 or 2 squares. They also only move right. They can jump over pieces now, though.

Kings can't go to C2

This rule is as simple as it sounds. Kings are simply unable to move to the C2 square in any situation.
