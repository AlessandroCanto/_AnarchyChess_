Confused? If so, try these 3 things

Learn the rules

If you're having trouble in games or are finding yourself confused by moves made, you might not understand all the new rules. By visiting the rules page you can better understand how the game works and have more fun playing other people.

Play against yourself

While reading the rules can help you understand the game more, sometime you need to see rules in action to understand them. You can do this by playing against yourself in the offline mode.

Contact us

Still confused, or facing technical difficulties. Contact us by leaving a suggestion or emailing us and we will get back to you as quickly as possible. Generally we respond within 5-10 business days, so if after 10 business days you have not heard back feel free to reach out again.
